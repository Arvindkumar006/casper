Autonomous Liquidity Protocol

NexusVault

A modular, multi-agent AI swarm that autonomously evaluates tokenized real-world assets (RWA), executes compliance clearances, and settles liquidity positions on the Casper Testnet ledger.

Why NexusVault Swarm?

Bringing compliance and decision velocity to Decentralized Asset Financing.

⚠

The Problem

Traditional RWA underwriting is slow, paper-driven, and highly siloed. Capital stays idle while manual underwriters inspect registries, credit checks, and cross-border blacklists.

⚙

The Solution

An autonomous AI Swarm representing diverse institutional roles collaborating in real-time. Nodes validate parameters, audit reserves, size deployments, and settle transactions in milliseconds.

✓

Blockchain Settle

Allocations are secured programmatically on Casper using Odra smart contracts. No human intervention is needed, creating a trustless and deterministic transaction settlements trail.
